In contrast to many other add-ons, which use Google Web APIs for generating QR codes, this add-on works completely offline. This QR code generator puts your privacy first! 🔐 It does this by working offline, independently of any internet connection! Always. 👌 So easy, you can use it with one click! 👌 It has a radically simple, yet powerful user interface, allowing you to tweak many details in the settings. Nevertheless, it's really easy to use! For instance, you can just resize the QR code with your mouse by dragging and dropping. Being lightweight and thus having a small size it is also fast and easy to install – even on mobile connections. 📦 Generate a variety of QR codes! 📦 Generate colored QR codes, save QR codes or simply just use them to send a tab or other text from your desktop to your mobile! You have the choice! Your QR code will only be one click away and you can instantly start typing to change the QR code text! 📢 More features 📢
  • Puts your privacy first! Privacy is the default here, so it is generating QR codes offline.
  • Follows Firefox Photon Design to integrate smoothly into the design of your Firefox.
  • It's super simple and intuitive usable!
  • Uses up-to-date, great, and customizable QR code libraries to generate the QR codes.
  • You can generate and save QR codes as SVG or PNG images (Canvas)!
  • You can configure the size, color and other aspects of the QR code.
  • Use a shortcut (Ctrl+Shift+F10) for generating the QR code.
  • Generates QR codes from selected text on the website.
  • Has complete Unicode/UTF-8/Emoji support.
  • Looks good on desktop and mobile devices, i.e. it is responsive!
  • Translated in several languages already. You can contribute your own language!
  • Compatible with Firefox for Android
  • Settings are synced across devices.
  • Settings can be managed by your administrator.
